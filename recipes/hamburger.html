Hamburger Patty

Hamburger Recipe

A hamburger, or simply burger, is a food consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll.

Ingredients

  • Ground lean beef
  • 1 egg
  • Minced Onion
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix everything
  2. Grill it both sides
  3. Lay buns and place it in between
  4. Assemble it and eat
